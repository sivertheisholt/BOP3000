Request Data

If you would like to request a copy of the personal data you've given us over the lifetime of your account, you can do so by clicking the "Request" button.
  • It may take up to 14 days.
  • You can't make a request while a request is pending.
  • When your data is ready, you will recieve an email containing the data.
  • Do not change your email while the request is pending. The data will be sent to the email address you had when you first requested the data.
  • If you delete your account, the request will be cancelled.

Delete Account

When you delete your account, your profile, history, connections, followers and likes will be permanently removed. This can not be undone.
